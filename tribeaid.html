TribeAid

The technology sector has seen a major advance change and will continue to move forward enhancement with research and development. In the current scenario, the concept of the Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence is growing rapidly. The developed countries such as United States, China, South Korea, etc are moving ahead with the research and development for moving towards obtaining sustainable livelihood that will enhance the overall living standards of people but modern technological development are not limited to developed countries only but have expanded to developing countries such as India.

In India, the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology focuses on extended utilization of technology as well as the implementation process of Internet of Things and artificial intelligence in providing the essential Service to urban, rural as well as tribal areas and aims toward the development of sustainable livelihood.

The Tribe Connect, an integrated platform, sponsored by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, acts as a bridge to empower and facilitates the tribal community with advanced technology for sustainable livelihood development. Tribe Connect provides a channel for amplification of technological developments through new advanced technologies such as the Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence which aims on enhancing the living standards of the tribal community. Tribe connect, an integrated platform, enables software and aims at instruments or gadgets that are capable of monitoring, sensing, data assembly, inferring as well as decision making for the provision of sustainable living standards. The platform uses advanced Internet of things & Artificial intelligence technologies to transform a tribal village, "Fulkarra" is a small tribal village situated in Gariyaband Tehsil, Chhattisgarh, into a model smart village as a case study i.e. focus is to transform the current tribal village into a smart full-grown village.

THE EXISTING STRUGGLES OF TRIBAL COMMUNITY

 Lack of health and social care.

 Lack of knowledge of harvesting and Management System.

 Water quality management system.

 Illiteracy.

 Lack of E-governance

 Lack of awareness about Government welfare Policy.

 Lack of management of Mandi System.

 Lack of Platform for Talent Promotion.

 Lack of Employment.

 Need of connectivity with the Outside world.

 Adoption on outdated Tradition method for social, economic, health and Education welfare.

To overcome these problems and create an integrated smart eco tribal platform for achieving sustainable Tribal Livelihoods Tribe connect the platform to focus on eliminating major vital problems for livelihood development i.e. Tribal health and Social care, harvest & Management System, and Empowerment & Service Sector. THE MAJOR OUTCOME OF TRIBE CONNECT

HEALTH & SOCIAL CARE

 smart Anganwadi Kendra equipped with ICT based Instruments for children and pregnant women malnutrition care.

 Development of a mobile software application capable of detection of anemia in Pregnant Women and children.

 Growth of a mobile application, for a Telemedicine facility for primary health care and diagnosis of community, in the targeted area.

HARVEST & MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

 Establishment of software application for Farming-Disease prevention.

 Provision of instrument for timely irrigation information.

 Provision of gadget for Well water quality monitoring.

 Provision of disaster monitoring and alerting system by IoT connected devices.

E-SERVICES & EMPOWERMENT

 organization of ICT based network to promote education.

 ICT based of the E-Mandi platform for marketing the tribal products.

 Software application for the provision of basic e-Governance facility.

 Deployment of software application platform Local talent Promotion.

 Established communication with the outside world.

Platform Prototype Product

 Smart Aganwadi Kendra having an Integrated Health monitoring system through a Health kiosk/ Health ATM for preliminary diagnosis and identifies malnutrition of children and Pregnant Women. Health Kiosk/Health ATM measures health parameters using various sensors integrated as a single unit. Also, detection of anemia in Pregnant Women and children are achieved by provision Internet of Things and Artificial intelligence-enabled software mobile application. A telemedicine facility is incorporated to enhance the Health Index of villagers.

 Kisan Assist prototype for precision Agriculture will be developed by including sensors for detecting soil moisture, pH, conductivity, etc also an Intelligent software that is capable of collecting data from the developed system, analyze, and early predicting of disease.

 For irrigation of field and automatic water irrigation management systems through IoT-enabled devices and Smartphone applications. IoT enabled Devices of Well Water quality Monitoring Disaster monitoring and alerting system through IoT connected devices.

 E-services and Empowerment, An integrated one-stop solution platform for the Empowerment of Village people,ICT-based network. The prototype will be developed to enhance the Education & Services of tribal community.

 For the economic development of villagers, ICT based E-Mandi platform will be developed for marketing of tribal products provides information about the market price also alert them about demand and supply requirements which helps in the development of tribal Entrepreneurship.Tribe Connect facilitates a model prototype for Local Talent promotion and communication with the outside world.
