About Us

Tribe connect is an innovative integrated platform, developed by the sponsorship of the Ministry of Electronics and Information technology (MeitY), to provide sustainable Tribal livelihood by utilization of the latest technology Internet of Things, and Artificial Intelligence. The integrated platform is developed under the collaboration of three premier research and development Institutes of India, the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, and the International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur, under the expert supervision of research and development teams members of Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, and International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur. The Tribe Connect software and hardware gadgets and instruments development team consist of members having an expert view in Computer Science Engineering, Project Management, Electrical, and Electronics Engineering as well Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Ministry Of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology is an associative agency of the Government of India which empowers as well as promotes e-governance, innovation, research, and development for substantial growth in the field of electronics, information technology, and electronic Industries. Currently, MeitY is operating under the Leadership of Shri Ravi Shankar Prasad, Minister of Electronics and Information Technology.

IIT Kanpur is one of the premier institutes declared as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under the Institutes of Technology Act.
